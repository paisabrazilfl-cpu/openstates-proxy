# Tawheed: the oneness of Allah

Tawheed is the central belief of Islam: Allah is one, unique, and alone worthy of worship. Muslims believe Allah has no partners, no equals, and no children. A short Quranic summary is Surah Al-Ikhlas: Allah is One, self-sufficient, neither begetting nor begotten, and nothing is comparable to Him.

# Worship belongs to Allah alone

In Islamic teaching, worship includes prayer, supplication, reliance, sacrifice, love, fear, and obedience in the religious sense. Dawah should invite people to worship the Creator rather than created things, while speaking respectfully and without coercion.
