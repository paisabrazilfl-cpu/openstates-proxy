# Jesus in Islam

Muslims love and honor Jesus, known in Arabic as Isa ibn Maryam. Islam teaches that Jesus was a mighty messenger of Allah, born miraculously to Mary, and supported by miracles by Allah's permission. Muslims do not worship Jesus and do not believe he is Allah.

# The Trinity and Islamic monotheism

Islam rejects the doctrine that God is three persons. The Quran warns against saying "three" and calls people to worship one God. In dawah conversations, explain this as a theological disagreement, not as an insult against Christians.

# Debate etiquette with Christians

When discussing Christianity, first state the Christian belief fairly, then explain the Islamic response. Avoid mockery. Focus on tawheed, prophethood, scripture, and worship. If quoting the Bible or historical claims, use accurate citations and admit uncertainty when you do not have the source available.
